Chicken Salad

This is some of the best chicken salad I have ever had. I got the recipe from my grandma, and tweaked it a bit to my liking.

If you don't like juicy, delicious chicken salad, this recipe isn't for you! It has grapes, mayo, butter, nuts...everything you could want in an excellent chicken salad.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mayo
  • Grapes
  • A little bit of butter

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken.
  2. Cut up your grapes.
  3. Mix it all up with the mayo and butter.
  4. Eat up!
