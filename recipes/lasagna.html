Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna (So Says Me)

In all my many travels across the globe, no recipe eluded me like that of the fabled Italian lasagna. Oh sure, I tried my hand at it from time to time, but never seemed to get it just right. Well my friends, all that changed once I found this recipe.

Just wait until you taste this stuff. It will change your life. You won't know what hit you. You won't know how you ever made it through existence on this mortal coil before it. It will in al likelihood make you have to sit down like Elaine trying the Soup Nazi's soup. So, without further ado, here is the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 8 sheets of lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar red pasta sauce
  • 1 8 oz container of ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound of italian ground sausage
  • 2 large eggs
  • Onions, peppers, and mushrooms

Steps

  1. Cook the sausage and vegetables in a pan until done.
  2. Boil your noodles until soft, but not too soft.
  3. Lay noodles out side by side in a casserole dish.
  4. Mix ricotta with eggs and spread a layer of the mixture over the noodles.
  5. Pour a layer of sauce over that.
  6. Repeat steps until lasagna is stacked high, to the ceiling.
  7. Bake in the oven until done. Just guess at the temperature.
